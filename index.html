About the 8th Air Force

Join the legendary 8th Air Force, the "Mighty Eighth," where strategic bombing defines victory. Command powerful bomber squadrons to strike deep into enemy territory, crippling key targets and turning the tide of war. Operate iconic aircraft, execute daring missions, and prove your precision and courage under fire. Be part of the force that dominates the skies—join the 8th Air Force and make history.

High Ranks of the 8th Air Force

General (O-10)

The highest rank, responsible for overall command and strategic oversight of the 8th Air Force.

Lieutenant General (O-9)

Senior leadership, overseeing major operations and coordinating bomber squadrons.

Major General (O-8)

Manages key divisions and ensures mission success through tactical planning.

Brigadier General (O-7)

Leads specific wings or groups, executing high-stakes bombing missions.

Colonel (O-6)

Commands squadrons, ensuring precision and discipline in operations.

Lieutenant Colonel (O-5)

Assists in squadron leadership and mission execution.
